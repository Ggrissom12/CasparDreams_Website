Art Portfolio

The above art is a highlight of my favorite pieces I have done from early 2019-onward. I will constantly update this page with more and more of my favorite pieces as I develop them. I do a mix of fanart, commissioned pieces, and original pieces for my own characters. My style is heavily influenced by old cartoons such as Looney Tunes, Ren and Stimpy, and even some newer artists who have taken a similar inspiration and develeoped it into their own style, such as Max Gilardi and Joshua Palmer. I plan on learning animation, and hope to see animations make their way onto this portfolio soon.
